Iskender Kebab

A Symphony of Taste and Texture

Immerse yourself in a symphony of succulent flavors and tantalizing textures with our Iskender Kebab. Crafted to perfection, tender slices of premium, meticulously marinated grilled meat are lovingly layered atop a bed of fluffy pita bread. A harmonious medley of roasted tomatoes, vibrant peppers, and sweet onions adds depth to each bite, creating a tapestry of taste that dances on your palate.

Flame-Grilled Authenticity

Our Iskender Kebab is born from time-honored traditions, embracing the essence of Turkish culinary artistry. Each piece of meat is carefully charred over an open flame, infusing it with a smoky essence that pays homage to centuries of skilled craftsmanship.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced grilled meat (usually lamb or beef)
  • Pita bread
  • Tomato sauce or tomato paste
  • Yoghurt
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Roasted tomatoes
  • Roasted green peppers
  • Sweet onions

  4. Layer the Flavors
  5. Finish and Serve
